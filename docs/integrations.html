Integration Setup Guide

  1. Log in to your NeuroLaunch dashboard.
  2. Click on the menu and go to Settings > Integrations.
  3. Select the app you want to integrate (e.g., Slack, Notion, Zapier).
  4. Click Connect and authorize access.
  5. Configure any custom rules or AI triggers.
  6. Click Save and test the integration.
